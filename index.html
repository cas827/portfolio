About Me

I am a Computer Science student at Lehigh University (Class of '26) with a passion for software engineering, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. I specialize in bridging the gap between complex backend data and clear, user-friendly frontend visualizations.

As an IT Intern at the Port Authority of NY & NJ, I developed procurement web portals, organized enterprise server configurations, and built cybersecurity monitoring dashboards. My role as the Data Editor for The Brown & White further honed my ability to translate technical requirements into engaging, interactive reporting for a broad audience.

Beyond coding, I strongly emphasize clean documentation, process improvement, and agile project management. I actively leverage tools like Atlassian Jira to manage development sprints, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and ensure operational continuity from end to end.
